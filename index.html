Crystal Collectors!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.
There are 4 crystals below. Clicking each will add a specfic point value to your total score.
You win by matching your total score to the goal number. You lose if you go over.
The value of each crystal in hidden until you click on it. All of the values will change with each new game.
